Medium wave antennas function in the medium frequency range, typically for commercial radio stations. Shortwave antennas cover a broad range of frequencies used for international broadcasting. Ultrashort wave antennas operate in the high-frequency range, often used for communication and broadcasting. Lastly, microwave antennas handle high-frequency signals used for wireless communication and satellite communication.</p></p>
