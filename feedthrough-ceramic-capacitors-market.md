<p><p>Feedthrough ceramic capacitors are a type of electronic component used to block, filter, and store electrical energy. The market for feedthrough ceramic capacitors can be categorized into two types: DC feedthrough ceramic capacitors and AC feedthrough ceramic capacitors. </p><p>DC feedthrough ceramic capacitors are designed for use in direct current (DC) applications, providing high insulation resistance and low leakage current. They are suitable for applications such as power supplies and motor drives.</p><p>AC feedthrough ceramic capacitors, on the other hand, are specifically designed for alternating current (AC) applications, offering high capacitance and low impedance. They are commonly used in electronic equipment, telecommunications, and power distribution systems.</p></p>
