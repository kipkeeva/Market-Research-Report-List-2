<p><p>Flat antennas are a type of antenna that are thin and lightweight, making them suitable for various applications. Electronically-steered antennas use electrical signals to dynamically control the direction of the antenna beam, allowing for efficient communication with multiple targets. Mechanically-steered antennas, on the other hand, physically rotate or move to change the direction of the antenna beam. Both types offer advantages such as improved signal quality, increased range, and faster data transmission, making them popular choices in industries like telecommunications, aerospace, and military.</p></p>
