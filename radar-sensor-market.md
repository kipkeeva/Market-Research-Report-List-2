<p><p>The radar sensor market consists of various types of sensors. Imaging radar uses radio waves to create detailed images of objects or landscapes for applications such as autonomous driving. CW (continuous wave) radar emits a continuous radio wave to measure distance, speed, or direction in applications like speed gauges. Pulse radar emits short bursts of radio waves and measures the time taken for the waves to bounce back, commonly used in non-imaging radar systems. Altimeter radar measures altitude by detecting the time taken for radio waves to reflect off the ground or objects.</p></p>
