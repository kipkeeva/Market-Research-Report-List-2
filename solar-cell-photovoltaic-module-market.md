<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The solar cell module market can be categorized into several types. Mono-Si modules are made from monocrystalline silicon, offering high efficiency but higher production costs. Multi-Si modules are made from multicrystalline silicon, providing lower efficiency but lower costs. CdTe modules are made from cadmium telluride thin film, commonly used for large-scale installations due to their cost-effectiveness. CIGS modules are made from copper indium gallium selenide, offering better efficiency and flexibility. a-Si modules are made from amorphous silicon, known for their lower costs but lower efficiency. Lastly, other modules include emerging technologies such as perovskite and organic solar cells.</p></p>
