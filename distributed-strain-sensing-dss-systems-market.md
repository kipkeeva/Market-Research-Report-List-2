<p><p>Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS) Systems are used to measure and monitor strain or deformation in structures like pipelines, bridges, and dams. There are two types of DSS systems: Single Mode Fiber-Based DSS and Multimode Fiber-Based DSS. Single Mode Fiber-Based DSS market involves using a single-mode optical fiber for strain measurement, offering high accuracy and long sensing distances. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber-Based DSS market uses multiple modes in optical fibers, providing cost-effective solutions for shorter sensing ranges and less accurate measurements.</p></p>
