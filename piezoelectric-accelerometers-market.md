<p><p>Piezoelectric Accelerometers are used to measure the vibrations and shocks in various applications. PE Type refers to the traditional piezoelectric accelerometer, where a charge is generated due to the applied force, and an external charge amplifier is required to measure the output. On the other hand, IEPE Type (Integrated Electronics Piezoelectric) accelerometers have a built-in charge amplifier, making them more convenient and easier to use. These accelerometers convert the mechanical stress into a voltage, which can directly be measured and amplified without the need for external devices.</p></p>
